Гражданин Земли

Gritty Caucasian male rap, deep raspy voice, heavy Russian accent, Middle Eastern melodic synth, Phrygian dominant scale, Darbuka percussion, EDM drop, Slow flow switching to fast double-time rap, Deep bass, Ethnic Trap, Cinematic ethnic atmosphere.

[Intro: Deep atmospheric pads, sounds of wind, a lonely Duduk playing a Middle Eastern melody]
