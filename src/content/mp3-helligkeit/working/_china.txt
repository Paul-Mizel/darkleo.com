Indian folk fusion featuring a prominent Shehnai melody. The Shehnai performs highly ornamented, microtonal phrases with rapid trills and glissandi. A high-pitched, metallic percussion instrument provides a steady, rapid rhythmic pulse in a fast 4/4 time signature. The arrangement is sparse, focusing on the interplay between the melodic wind instrument and the percussive drive. Key of C# Major at 145 BPM.
